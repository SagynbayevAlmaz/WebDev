Recipe #1 Crab salad

  • Time: 30min
  • Serves: 3min
Ingredients Quantity
Crab stick 1 package
Cucumber 2 pieces
Corn 1 can
Eggs 6 pieces

Step 1: Boil Eggs for 15 min.

Step 2: Cut all ingredients.

Step 3: Season with mayonnaise.

Recipe #2 Omlet

  • Time: 10min
  • Serves: 2min
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 3 pieces
Milk 30ml

Step 1: Mix eggs and milk.

Step 2: Season with salt and pepper.

Step 3: fry in a pan until tender.

Recipe #3 Sandwich

  • Time: 25min
  • Serves: 5min
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 1 peice
Bread 2 peice
Tomatoes 2 peices
Salad 2 peices

Step 1: Cut the breast in half, season with salt and pepper.

Step 2: Fry the breast in the pan until tender.

Step 3: Make toasts in pan.

Step 4: Put it all together.
